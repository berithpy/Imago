## Context
Ratings are most useful when actionable through filtering and export.

## Goal
Add rating filters and support rating in both grid and lightbox views.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Filters: all, good, bad, neutral, disagreement.
- [ ] Disagreement behavior is defined and implemented consistently.
- [ ] Exports can honor selected rating filter.
- [ ] Viewer sees own vote in grid and lightbox.

## Notes
Viewer visibility of other voters remains a product toggle decision.
